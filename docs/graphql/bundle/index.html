The LiftwizardGraphQLBundle extends com.smoketurner.dropwizard.graphql.GraphQLBundle .The bundle registers https://github.com/graphql/graphiql the GraphIQL UI at /graphiql and https://github.com/graphql/graphql-playground the GraphQL Playground UI at /graphql-playground , by delegating to AssetsBundle . This overrides the behavior of the smoketurner bundle, which registers just one UI at / (graphiql in older versions, and graphql-playground in newer versions).The bundle also registers two instrumentations for logging and metrics.
